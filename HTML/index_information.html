On this website you can find out how many criminal activities are commited for each country in the world. The criminal activities are divided in the following subgroups: assaults, kidnappings, thefts, robberies, burglaries and sexual violences.

This visualizations is designed to collect data of reported crimes with the goal to improve the spread of that information. It is visualized by a map of the world with different colors indicating the amount of crimes per 100.000 inhabitants. You can click on some countries, whereafter you can see a bar plot of the different types of criminal activities and a pie chart with the gender of the criminal.
